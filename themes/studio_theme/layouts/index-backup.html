New-build gardens face unique challenges

With all the focus on interior finishes, many new-build gardens in Bedfordshire and Buckinghamshire are overlooked by neighbours, consist of fence to fence turf and a tiny patio.

You should be able to:

  • enjoy a coffee in the garden without feeling watched,
  • entertain friends and family in a comfortable, relaxing space, and
  • appreciate the seasonality and beauty of a well-designed garden.

Instead, you're stuck with a garden that feels exposed and uninspiring - hardly the outdoor sanctuary that should complement your beautiful new home.

I help new-build owners transform these overlooked spaces into exceptional gardens that create privacy, support local wildlife, and provide the wow-factor to match your contemporary home.

Wyld Design creates gardens that:

  • are buzzing with life,
  • have the wow-factor to match your home, and
  • soften the hard edges of new-build houses with flowing, seasonal planting.
