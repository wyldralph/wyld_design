Contemporary Garden Design That Transforms New-Build Homes

Creating naturalistic gardens that support local wildlife through sustainable design and expert horticulture.

New-build gardens are overlooked, in more ways than one...

With all focus on interior finishes, most new-build gardens remain underdeveloped spaces - offering little more than basic lawns and minimal hard landscaping .

Your outdoor space should give you:

Tranquil privacy for peaceful morning rituals
Sophisticated entertaining areas for memorable gatherings
Year-round visual interest through expertly curated planting

Instead, you're left with a garden that feels exposed and uninspiring - hardly the outdoor sanctuary that should complement your beautiful new home.

I collaborate with homeowners who value exceptional design and environmental stewardship, creating contemporary gardens that celebrate local wildlife whilst reflecting their unique lifestyle.

Wyld Design gardens are:

Architectural in presence to complement your home
Layered with seasonal planting that softens contemporary lines
Abundant with wildlife

How I can help you.

Garden Design

Transform your outdoor space from concept to completion. Perfect for new-build gardens which are a blank slate, or established gardens requiring total transformation. I'll guide you through the process, creating a cohesive, luxury garden that complements your home while supporting local wildlife.

Planting Design

Breathe new life into your existing garden with expert plant selection and seasonal schemes. Ideal if your hard landscaping works well but your planting feels uninspiring, struggling, or lacks the wow-factor to match your home. I'll create naturalistic planting designs that provide year-round interest and attract wildlife.

Design Consultation

Get expert guidance and a clear direction for your garden project. Perfect if you're overwhelmed by possibilities, want to tackle improvements yourself, or need professional advice before committing to major changes. Includes a detailed written report with recommendations and next steps tailored to your space and budget.
